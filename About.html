Designer, iOS developer and other nouns...

Hi, my name is Augusto Paixão, I'm known as Paixão or Paixi. I'm crazy about studies and so far I've managed to get a degree in film and a master's degree in design, as well as a multitude of online courses. I'm currently at the very end of my second degree, which is in design.

Since 2017 I have been working on developing interfaces and interactive experiences, focusing especially on Apple systems. One experience that fully guided me in this direction was the Apple Developer Academy, a hands-on learning project I participated in between 2018 and 2019.

My academic research interests revolve mainly around the small components of everyday life, gestures, details, and interpretation of life itself as an artistic process. In the last few years, I've been mainly dedicated to IDKY Apps, which is the startup I created to organize all my apps, being them from the education category.
