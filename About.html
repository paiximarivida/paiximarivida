Hello, my name is Passion!

I am a designer, developer, and filmmaker from Recife, Brazil. I love creating experiences that enrich people's lives!!

I graduated from the Apple Developer Academy in design, development, and entrepreneurship. I also completed a bachelor's degree in design and audiovisual, as well as a master's degree in design. All three courses were at the Federal University of Pernambuco.

I've been working in design since 2017, focusing on products for banking and the financial market, education, and health.

My main interests in design are: usability, UX research, interfaces, Figma, UX writing, data analysis, design systems, prototyping, A/B testing, web design, artificial intelligence and handoffs.
